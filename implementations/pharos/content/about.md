# About Pharos-SSG

Pharos-SSG is part of the poly-ssg ecosystem, demonstrating static site generation in Pharo Smalltalk.

## Why Pharo Smalltalk?

Pharo offers a live, image-based development environment where everything is an object. Its reflective capabilities and powerful IDE enable rapid prototyping and debugging. Pharos-SSG showcases Smalltalk's elegant object model for representing content, templates, and build pipelines.
