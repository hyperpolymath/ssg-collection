# About baremetal-ssg

baremetal-ssg is part of the poly-ssg ecosystem, demonstrating static site generation in x86-64 Assembly.

## Why x86-64 Assembly?

Assembly provides ultimate control over the machine. baremetal-ssg proves that even without high-level abstractions, you can build practical web tooling. It serves as an educational exploration of what happens at the lowest level of computing, making every byte count.
