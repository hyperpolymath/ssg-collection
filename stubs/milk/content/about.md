# About milk-ssg

milk-ssg is part of the poly-ssg ecosystem, demonstrating static site generation in COW.

## Why COW?

COW is a Brainfuck variant where instructions are represented by variations of "moo". It demonstrates that even the most esoteric languages can power practical tools like static site generators. The whimsical nature of COW makes milk-ssg a fun exploration of language design.
